APPROACH

Unlike in the conventional approach, we make using the language the focal point from the beginning. You accumulate vocab and grammar progressively as you need it, rather than being bombarded with it before it is relevant. This is an inductive learning approach, moving from the specific to the general. So when you discover the general rule, it is immediately useful and you’re primed to grasp it easily. A tutor leads you to continually work out and expand your abilities (like in the gym). Your motivation is supercharged from feeling real progress.
